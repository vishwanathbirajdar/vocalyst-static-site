Assessment Modules
Speech Assessment
Evaluate spoken fluency, clarity, pronunciation, and accent strength as a candidate responds to real workplace topics.
Problem: High attrition in call centers due to misfit hires.
How Vocalyst helps: Accurately screens for real-world spoken ability, reducing mismatches and training costs.
Repeat After Me
Test listening skills and immediate recall by asking candidates to repeat professional phrases and instructions.
Problem: Employees misunderstand or forget key client instructions.
How Vocalyst helps: Ensures new hires have strong active listening and accurate echoing in real-time.
Storytelling Assessment
Analyze a candidate’s natural communication, creativity, structure, and engagement by having them narrate stories on-the-spot.
Problem: Soft skills and creative thinking are hard to measure.
How Vocalyst helps: Identifies talent for leadership and customer engagement where canned answers fall short.
Pronunciation Assessment
Pinpoint pronunciation accuracy on industry, brand, or general vocabulary—critical for clear customer communication.
Problem: Mispronounced brand/product names harm customer trust.
How Vocalyst helps: Flags critical words/pronunciations before hiring, avoiding post-hire correction.
Free Speech
Assess verbal confidence and adaptability by presenting candidates with unexpected topics for an impromptu response.
Problem: Recruits struggle in unpredictable phone/video interactions.
How Vocalyst helps: Validates spontaneous language ability for high-contact or escalation roles.
Conversation Simulation
Simulate real client or colleague conversations, measuring response accuracy, etiquette, and empathy in high-pressure settings.
Problem: Candidates score well in written tests but falter in real dialogue.
How Vocalyst helps: Measures practical, on-the-fly communication needed for support and sales.
Reading Passage
Measure reading fluency, clarity, and accent in formal/business contexts using predefined technical or business scripts.
Problem: Fluency with business language is required but tough to judge at scale.
How Vocalyst helps: Standardizes reading out loud of critical materials for large teams.
Visual Prompt Speech
Candidates describe or interpret images and visual prompts, demonstrating vocabulary, descriptive detail, and organization.
Problem: Descriptive abilities are key for training, onboarding, or field roles.
How Vocalyst helps: Assesses if a candidate can clearly convey detailed information or observations.
Role Play
Put candidates into simulated workplace scenarios to test their approach to negotiation, persuasion, or problem resolution.
Problem: Customer or escalation scenarios require quick judgment & empathy.
How Vocalyst helps: Simulated exercises highlight best-fit personality and skill mix.
Listening Comprehension
Candidate listens to an audio clip and answers questions to test comprehension, retention, and ability to execute verbal instructions.
Problem: Task errors arise from poor instruction retention.
How Vocalyst helps: Directly tests comprehension of both simple and complex spoken material.
Error Correction
Present intentionally incorrect text or audio and ask candidates to find and correct mistakes.
Problem: Quality assurance, auditing, and training require strong correction skills.
How Vocalyst helps: Identifies those with precise attention to detail.
Stress/Intonation Analysis
AI analyzes natural use of pitch, stress, and intonation for engaging, fatigue-free voice profiles in customer support.
Problem: Monotone, flat delivery causes poor customer interaction and faster burnout.
How Vocalyst helps: Selects voices that are clear, pleasant, and engaging for any shift.
Multilingual Assessment
Test a candidate’s ability to use multiple languages or accents within the same assessment, for versatile and global teams.
Problem: Multinational or multilingual BPOs need nimble communicators.
How Vocalyst helps: Validates code-switching and language accuracy before hiring.
